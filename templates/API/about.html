About the API

The diversityorgs.tech API is designed with two missions:

  • Provide Easy Access for Admins to update organizations
  • Allow organizations and other sites the ability to use our organization lists on their pages as it makes sense.
    •

    DiversityOrgs.Tech was designed not to be the final stop on your journey in discovering community. Also, for many people this site won't be the first either.

    As an aggregator, the goal of this site is to make it easier for people to find organizaitons they believe focus on them.
